Aryan Rose

Participant

Hi there! My name is Aryan Rose and I was a participant at the inaugural Inspire Science competition. This experience was both challenging and enriching. It gave me and other students the chance to take our scientific knowledge and apply in different real-life contexts through debate, public speaking and academic writing. I was very impressed by the wide range of students of different nationalities and ages that came together to contribute to a competitive and learning environment. To all young aspiring scientists out there, Inspire Science gives you the opportunity to pursue and learn more about your interests and I personally cannot wait for next year's competition!

Sara Wilsson

Designer

The Inspire Science Competition was one of the most unique, creative and enlightening events I have been to. The way the concept of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math was propelled in this competition was phenomenal. The competitions inside the event are so vast and creative that the experience is unparalleled. I enjoyed the writing event because teams and students had the chance to research a scientific topic prior to the event which circulated around the fields of Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. The public speaking event allowed the creation of an environment that is very fond of learning and allowed for a better scientific understanding of the real-life applications that science gives to the world. This allowed me to see how the world can tackle predominant issues such as climate change using science. My favorite event was the debate because we were able to portray our scientific knowledge and perspective and we could see how it differs from someone else’s. The sponsors of the event were IDP and Penrose Learning Centre. Prior to the event, I had never ever come across these companies but by the end of the event, I was extremely inspired to give it a go. Being 12, I am very passionate about a lot of subjects and fields including computer science, and after I had heard that Penrose learning Centre has some courses regarding coding I was extremely open to trying programs in both IDP and Penrose. The Inspire Science competition has advertised the sponsors really well by integrating them into both the opening and closing ceremony. After the competition, I was extremely passionate and had a willingness to join the sponsors’ programs thanks to the Inspire Science team. I believe because of the Inspire Science Competition a lot more students who participate know about the sponsors. I would like to give my gratitude to the Inspire Science team as well as the adjudicators, sponsors and faculty staff for creating such a memorable experience in my academic life. I would like to see this event to bring more and more enthusiastic learners in the field of science for the many more years to come.

Jena Karlis

Store Owner

My experience in the Inspire Science competition was indeed a memorable one. This competition was all-encompassing of the various fields in the sciences, while aptly touching upon pressing issues in our world today. It was a competition that kept its participants on their toes, and definitely allowed me to challenge myself to achieve so much more. I hadn’t heard of these sponsors up until the Inspire Science competition, but I am now aware of their goals and aims and I am considering contacting and getting involved with one of them (Penrose) to participate in an interesting coding session. Therefore, I believe that the Inspire Science team indeed did a great job of advertising them and clearly stating to us what they hope to achieve by increasing their target audience and their influence on the younger generation. Overall, it was truly an honor to be conferred with the “Best Team” award along with my teammate Aryan Gulati. We had a great time and definitely learned so much more about the science world.
